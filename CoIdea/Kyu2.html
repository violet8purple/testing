Brewing instructions

  1. Tear along the dotted line to open the coffee bag.
  2. Pull out the hanging arms and place the bag on top of a mug.
  3. Pour hot water over the grounds.
  4. Leave it for 2 minutes, dispose of the coffee bag, and enjoy the coffee.
